Your Data Privacy
What data is collected?
This website collects and stores your responses to all 12 questions and the calculations based on those answers.
Why is the data collected?
We collect your question answers so we can calculate your style and personalize your browsing experience.
Where is the data stored?
Your data is stored on your local browser in a cookie. It is not stored anywhere else.
Is my data encrypted?
No, your data is not encrypted. Encryption might come later, but for now this prototype site does not include it.
How can I delete my data?
By default, your data is automatically deleted when you fully close your browser. You can also manually clear your browser cookies, or use the button below.
